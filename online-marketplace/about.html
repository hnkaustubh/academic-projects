About Us

GoTech Smart Solutions has been developing markets and supports technology platforms for secondary loan businesses since 1999. Our system address the full matrix of mortgage products, with products reaching from loan acquisition and financing through servicer surveillance, securitization, and loan accounting.

Our client roster list some of the largest financial institutions in the country, including investment banks, hedge funds, and mortgage companies. In addition, we have a close working relationship with over 30 service providers in the mortgage industry.

Gotech Smart Solutions' highly experienced development and support team possess in-depth knowledge of the mortgage industry. Our staff brings together experience from a diverse range of fields ranging from trading and investment banking, to top tier IT consulting and academia. Our state of the art hosted infrastructure, combines with our capital markets expertise, lets our clients avoid technology hurdles and start doing business better and faster.
